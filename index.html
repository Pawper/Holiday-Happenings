This is for You

The holidays can be a rough experience for many folks who do not have good relationships with their families. The reality for a lot of people, especially LGBTQ ks, is that if they do not have holiday plans with friends, they do not have any holiday plans. Unfortunately, too many times the healthy choice is to not join family gatherings that stress you out.

This project is dedicated to you and your new gaming friends, uniting for some TTRPG magic and bringing holiday cheer to those who need it most.

A Special 25 Day Event

Holiday Happenings runs from December 1st through December 24th. This is a moment in which there is a space for you to spread joy to those in need—through festive TTRPG content. On December 25th, the PDF will be available at the Holiday Happenings Itch page.
