This Is For You

As you may or may not know, the holidays can be a rough experience for many folks who do not have good relationships with their families. The reality for a lot of people, especially LGBTQ folks, is that if they do not have holiday plans with friends, they do not have holiday plans. And unfortunately, too many times the wise choice is to not join family situations that stress you out.

This project is dedicated to you and your new gaming friends, uniting for some TTRPG magic and bringing holiday cheer to those who need it most.
