OnTheXDay Challenge

24 days of finding joy in drawing.

Time to cherish a pen, pencil or stylus. Anyone can join the OnTheXDay challenge.

Draw for Holiday Happenings every day starting December 1st and ending December 24th.

How It Works:

  1. Doodle, draw or illustrate for Holiday Happenings content (check out the draft in progress).
  2. Post it to Twitter with the hashtag #HolidayHappenings.
  3. Link to the tweet in #on-the-x-day-art-challenge on the Holiday Happenings Discord.
  4. If you want to submit any art for publication in Holiday Happenings, please read the participation rules and submit each piece to #submission-box on the Holiday Happenings Discord.
  5. Do it again the next day.
